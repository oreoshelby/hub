About

THM 2022

    Travancore Hub Meet is the annual flagship event of IEEE Travancore Hub of IEEE LINK, the vibrant student activities community of IEEE Kerala Section. The meet sees an annual participation of students from across the length and breadth of the travancore hub (Trivandrum, Kollam & Pathanamtitta). THM is a techno-managerial event that furnishes to enhance and update technical knowledge of undergraduate participants from all over the 3 districts. Nothing beats a meeting of adroitness, amusement and wisdom and that is what IEEE Travancore Hub tries to achieve through its most prestigious event, the IEEE TRAVANCORE HUB MEET, a place to learn, a place to grow and a chance to peep into the insights of your dreams. The 2-day event predominantly provides an opportunity for the student community to meet highly intelligent and motivated peer, enhance their skills through trainings, workshops, and competitions and gear them up to face the professional career ahead of them. The fact that enlightens about THM is not merely the advanced workshops but also the unique alliance of students and industry experts, who are united together to celebrate the joy of knowledge.

THM

A Recall

2015

The 2015 version of Travancore Hub Meet named CONVENTUS was held at Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology Trivandrum on July 25th & 26th, 2015. This event had seen a participation over 300 from different colleges. A total of 10 sessions amd 4 workshops were conducted. The Event brought together students from various colleges in Trivandrum, Kollam and Pathanamthitta. About 300 Students from 15 different colleges participated in this event. A number of workshops were organised, including Arduino, Raspberry pi, Robotics, Photoshop and MATLAB. The workshops received overwhelming and enthusiastic participation from all the students.

2017

THM 2017 was held at Marian Engineering College Trivandrum from 04th - 06th August 2017. It had seen a participation over 300 from more than 20 different Colleges. The event provided opportunity for the participants to enhance the technical and managerial skills of students invoking creativity and innovation. These skills helped them strengthen theirr human resource capabilities that can make them a leader in the 21st century, where mankind gives paramount importance to knowledge which is backed up by technology. By providing a platform for interaction, learning and networking, THM was able to inculcate such a mind-set which is very valuable in today's context where technology is constantly in a flux.

2018

In 2018, THM was held at College of Engineering Pathanapuram from 05th - 07th October 2018. The event served as a wonderful platform to enhance their technical knowledge and also to increase the interest in volunteering.

2019

THM 2019 was held at TKM College of Engineering Kollam from 11th - 13th October 2019. The 3-day tech summit conducted by IEEE, the world's largest professional organisation, became the venue for a wide variety of workshops, lecture sessions and competitions. This year's THM hit a record registration of over 300 and it became a platform for the participants to interact with experts from various technical fields. The cultural evenings made the summit more enthralling.

IEEE

Kerala Section

   With 14000+ members, IEEE Kerala Section is one of the most vibrant communities in the ranks of IEEE. The Section ensures growth of technical prowess, managerial skills and volunteering spirit among its members. It is the largest among 343 sections of the IEEE spread across 165 countries. home to a vibrant volunteer community carrying out technical activities in the state. IEEE Kerala section is home to a vibrant volunteer community carrying out technical activities in the state, including the rapid development of "keralarescue.in" during the Kerala Floods of 2018. It is also well known globally, with many of its activities and achievements garnering global attention and being replicated across other Sections at the global level.

IEEE Hub

IEEE Travancore Hub

   IEEE Travancore Hub was formed as part of IEEE LINK (Local Integrated Network of Kerala) in the year 2005. Travancore Hub Meet is the annual flagship event of IEEE TRAVANCORE HUB, in which participants from various student branches across Travancore Hub gather. THM has always been a conclave for bringing together the youth, professionals, and officials with their ideologies and experiences. It provides an opportunity for the student community to meet highly intelligent and motivated peers, enhance their skills through training and competitions and gear them up to face the professional career ahead of them. This year, we are planning to host THM’22 tentatively on 26th and 27th of November 2022 (2 days) with a total participation count of 250.

IEEE Student Branch

IEEE SB CET

   The College of Engineering Trivandrum is the first engineering college in Kerala, situated in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala. It was founded in 1939 during the reign of the Travancore King, Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma.Over the years, the intake of students increased and as of now with eight full-fledged departments offering eight undergraduate, 27 postgraduate and doctoral programmes under the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, the College has around 4500 students, 311 teaching staff and 290 non-teaching staff. B.Tech, B.Arch, M.Tech, M.Plan, MCA and MBA are also offered by the College. IEEE SB CET aim to provide a technical home, a place where each student member can develop and hone skills that will earmark them for prominent positions in their careers.Through volunteering in the variety of workshops and events that IEEE SB CET host, speaking in and attending chapter sessions, the members get to explore new realms of interest and adapt to different situations enabling them to easily fit into any technical or professional role, even after their student life.
