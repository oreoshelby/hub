About THM

   Travancore Hub Meet (THM) is the annual flagship event of IEEE Travancore Hub of IEEE LINK, the vibrant student activities community of IEEE Kerala Section. It is a techno-managerial event that furnishes to enhance and update technical knowledge of students from the districts of Trivandrum, Kollam and Pathanamthitta. Nothing beats a meeting of adroitness, amusement and wisdom; with a fair share of workshops, trainings, competitions and networking sessions, the 2-day event will gear them up to face the professional career ahead of them.

Our Host

IEEE SB CET

   The College of Engineering Trivandrum is the first engineering college in Kerala, situated in Thiruvananthapuram. IEEE SB CET aim to provide a technical home, a place where each student member can develop and hone skills that will earmark them for prominent positions in their careers.Through volunteering in the variety of workshops and events that IEEE SB CET host, speaking in and attending chapter sessions, the members get to explore new realms of interest and adapt to different situations enabling them to easily fit into any technical or professional role, even after their student life

An annual flagship event of IEEE Travancore Hub, the Travancore Hub Meet (THM) is a 2-day gathering of students and industry experts who unite together to espouse the power of knowledge. This serves as an opportunity for students to meet their peers, sharpen their skills (through the various training opportunities, workshops, and competitions), and prepare them to embrace the professional career ahead of them.

There are no restrictions on the events the participants can attend.

Food and accommodation will be provided.

THM’22 will be conducted on the 9th to 11th of December, 2022 at College of Engineering, Trivandrum.

The event is open to all.

Refund will not be provided unless decided otherwise by the Organising Committee.

Travancore Hub Meet (THM) is the annual flagship event of IEEE Travancore Hub of IEEE LINK, the vibrant student activities community of IEEE Kerala Section.
